advantages of working in big cities

  • More chances of meeting new people
  • A lot of variety and choices

  • More overall facilities and knowledge about health

disadvantages of working in big cities

  1. The cost of living is higher in the city than in suburban or rural areas
  2. The close quarters of subway cars and sidewalks aren’t for everyone, and it’s a significant change from life in a small town.
  3. The large population of major metro areas can create other unforeseen challenges in your daily life
  4. There’s nothing wrong with being sensitive to stress, and the magic of living in a big city will be lost on you if you end up frazzled by your day-to-day life.
